# Ó Caviar CMS - User Guide

## How to Access

1. Go to: `https://yoursite.netlify.app/admin/`
2. Log in with your Netlify credentials
3. The CMS dashboard will load automatically

## Creating a New Article

1. Click on **"Journal Articles"**
2. Click **"New Journal Articles"**
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Title**: Article headline
   - **Publish Date**: Publication date
   - **Excerpt**: Short description (appears on cards, max 150 characters)
   - **Hero Image**: Main article image (drag & drop, minimum 1200px wide)
   - **Content**: Article body (use the editor for formatting)
   - **Tags**: Keywords (maximum 5, use commas to separate)
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly name (lowercase, hyphens only, auto-generated from title)
4. Click **"Publish"**
5. Your article will appear on the site in 2-3 minutes

## Editing an Article

1. Select the article from the list
2. Make your changes
3. Click **"Publish"** to save

## Deleting an Article

1. Select the article
2. Click **"Delete"**
3. Confirm the action
4. The article will be removed from the site automatically

## Formatting Tips

### Text Formatting
- **Bold**: Select text and click the bold button
- *Italic*: Select text and click the italic button
- Links: Select text, click link button, paste URL
- Headings: Use ## for subtitles in your content

### Images
- Use high-quality images (JPG for photos, PNG for graphics)
- Keep file names simple, no spaces or special characters
- Images are automatically optimized for web

### Writing Best Practices
- Keep titles concise (max 60 characters)
- Write engaging excerpts (1-2 sentences)
- Use short paragraphs for better readability
- Add relevant tags consistently
- Leave blank lines between paragraphs

## What Happens Automatically

When you publish an article:
- ✅ Article is saved to the repository
- ✅ Individual page is created at `/journal/article-name/`
- ✅ Article appears in the journal list
- ✅ Homepage shows the 3 most recent articles
- ✅ SEO tags are generated automatically
- ✅ Mobile-responsive design applied

## Important Notes

- **Slug format**: Always lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `my-article-name`)
- **Hero image**: Required for every article
- **Auto-save**: The editor saves your work automatically
- **Preview**: You can preview your article before publishing
- **Changes are immediate**: Published articles appear on the site within minutes

If something isn't working as expected, contact your developer with details about what you're trying to do.
