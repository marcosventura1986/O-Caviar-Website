A Legacy of Excellence

At Ó Caviar, we meticulously mature the noble sturgeon's roe to its perfect peak, an expression defined by our signature affinage.

Heritage

Sturgeon, revered and respected

Rooted in lineage and purity, our farming partners embody a profound respect for the majestic sturgeon. They champion responsible stewardship through sustainable, ethical practices that safeguard the species and mimic its natural habitat. Every pearl of caviar carries the promise of full traceability—honouring transparency, care, and a commitment to preserving this ancient legacy for generations to come.

Affinage

Time, the alchemist's touch

Each pearl of Ó Caviar is patiently guided to perfection - texture harmonised, salinity balanced, aroma exquisitely refined. Through meticulous maturation, time transforms caviar into a symphony of flavour and elegance.

Exclusivity

Every tin of Ó Caviar is a curated masterpiece.

We select only the finest roe from each farm’s top production, where global expertise converges with uncompromising stewardship. Every batch undergoes meticulous scrutiny, a comprehensive sensory evaluation and rigorous quality control, earning approval only when it meets our exacting standards. This uncompromising selection ensures complete satisfaction for those who seek the pinnacle of caviar perfection.

The Art of Maturation

An elegant, deliberate journey from selection to presentation.

Selection

Sturgeon raised with care in pristine waters, respecting heritage and purity.

Harvest

At the precise moment, roe is harvested with optimal composition and fat content.

Curation

The fresh roe is cleaned of impurities then lightly salted (Malossol) to preserve its natural integrity. Only at this point can we call it Caviar.

Maturation

Ó Caviar's meticulous affinage allows each precious pearl to reveal its fullest, most harmonious expression, transforming the caviar into an unparalleled gastronomic masterpiece.

Presentation

Following careful packing and final quality checks, Ó Caviar is sealed in precision-engineered vacuum tins, removing air to lock in pristine aromas and delicate nuances. This airtight packaging preserves the caviar’s exquisite flavour and texture, ensuring an unrivalled sensory experience from the moment the tin is opened.
