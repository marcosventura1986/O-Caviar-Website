Cookies Policy

Last updated: November 3, 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Related technologies include local storage, pixels, and SDKs.

2. Who sets cookies?

  • First-party cookies are set by us.
  • Third-party cookies are set by other providers (e.g., analytics, advertising, social media).

3. Legal basis & consent (EU/EEA)

Under the ePrivacy Directive and GDPR, we may store cookies on your device only if they are strictly necessary for the operation of the Site. For all other cookies (e.g., analytics, marketing), we ask for your consent via our cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time.

4. How we use cookies

We may use the following categories:

  • Strictly necessary: Core functionality such as navigation, shopping cart, checkout, security. (No consent required.)
  • Preferences: Remembering your choices (e.g., language, region).
  • Analytics: Understanding how visitors use the Site to improve performance.
  • Marketing: Measuring campaigns, showing relevant ads, preventing ad fraud.
  • Social media: Enabling sharing and embedded content.

5. Managing cookies

  • Consent banner: Use the banner to accept/reject non-essential cookies and to change settings at any time.
  • Browser controls: You can block or delete cookies via your browser. Doing so may affect Site functionality.
  • Mobile device settings: Your device may provide settings to limit ad tracking.
  • Do Not Track: Our Site currently does not respond to DNT signals.

6. Third-party providers

Third-party providers may process your data for their own purposes as independent controllers. Review their policies:

  • Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will change accordingly.

8. Contact

Questions about cookies or your consent choices? Contact hello@ocaviar.com.
